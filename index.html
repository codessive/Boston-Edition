Watch It OutsideTM Boston Edition

Coming soon to this town, your favorite outdoor film festival: "Watch It Outside Boston Edition"!

Every evening, starting from the 5th to 8th of August, we'll be bringing you the best in movie entertainment. We've got action, comedy, fantasy, drama, horror, romance, all the genres and all packed with your favorite screen characters.

Thanks to Boston Garden & Parks, there will be seats for about 1500 people everyday, so make sure you register below to reserve your seat.

You can also check the Boston News for updates and schedules.
